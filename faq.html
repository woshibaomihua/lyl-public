Buyer FAQ

Air purifier OEM and sample order FAQ

These questions are written for real B2B buyers: MOQ, samples, certifications, factory audit, payment, packaging, filters, inspection and shipment.

Can LYL support private label air purifier orders with custom logo and packaging?

Yes. Buyers should provide brand name, logo files, packaging language, target country, estimated quantity and launch channel. LYL can then confirm which model, MOQ and packaging path fits the project.

What keywords should I use when requesting an OEM air purifier quotation?

Use clear procurement phrases such as OEM air purifier manufacturer China, private label air purifier, CE/FCC/RoHS air purifier supplier, large room HEPA air purifier, UV-C disinfection lamp cart or HVAC bipolar ionizer. These terms help the sales team understand your intended product direction.

Do you supply replacement HEPA filters for private label programs?

Replacement filter programs should be planned together with the main purifier SKU. Buyers can request filter SKU, replacement cycle, barcode, package design and refill pricing for repeat-order planning.

Which LYL product is suitable for HVAC or duct air purification projects?

The HVAC Air Purifier Duct Bipolar Ionizer Plasma Sterilization System is the most relevant direction for duct and project buyers. Project quotation should include installation context, quantity, documentation needs and target country.

Which LYL products fit UV-C disinfection wholesale demand?

The Commercial Grade UV-C Disinfection Lamp Cart and the 200W High Intensity UV-C Germ-Killing Lamp are relevant for commercial UV-C disinfection distributors and facility suppliers.

What is your MOQ for air purifier OEM orders?

MOQ depends on the model. The supplied product list includes MOQ examples from 1 pc for an office smoke odor purifier, 10 pcs for the HVAC duct ionizer system, and 50 pcs for several home air purifier and UV-C cart products.

Can I order samples before bulk production?

Yes. The sample request page collects product model, company name, WhatsApp, target country, quantity and required documents so the sales team can confirm sample cost, freight and available documents.

How long is sample preparation?

Sample timing should be confirmed by model and stock status. For a framework site, the correct approach is to ask the buyer for target deadline, plug type, market and document requirements before committing.

Can the sample cost be refunded after bulk order?

This should be negotiated by order size and sample configuration. The FAQ should not overpromise; it should invite buyers to discuss refund policy during RFQ.

Do you support private label logo and packaging?

Yes, OEM/ODM and private-label pathways are supported by the site structure. Buyers should provide logo file, packaging size, manual language, barcode requirements and target market.

Which certifications are available?

Reference material lists CE, FCC, EPA, GS, RoHS, ISO9001:2015 and CARB. Final certificate availability must be checked by exact model and SKU.

Can you provide CE or FCC certificates for my selected model?

Use the certification page or RFQ form to request model-specific documents. Certification support should be tied to model number, plug version and target country.

Can I arrange a factory audit or video inspection?

Yes. The framework includes future factory tour and inspection paths. Buyers can request video inspection, factory photos, production line photos or third-party inspection support.

What payment terms do you accept?

Final terms should be confirmed in quotation. B2B buyers usually discuss sample payment, deposit before production and balance before shipment or after inspection.

Can you customize plug type and manual language?

For export orders, plug type, voltage, label language, manual language and carton marks should be confirmed before sampling and mass production.

Do you support replacement filter private label?

For home purifier models, replacement filter SKU planning is important. Buyers can request private-label filter packaging, barcode and replenishment terms.

How often should filters be replaced?

Replacement cycle depends on filter design, usage environment and local air quality. The supplier should provide model-specific recommendations and filter SKU support.

Do you support Amazon or retail packaging?

Private-label and retail packaging should be confirmed by MOQ, box dimensions, compliance labels and marketplace requirements.

Can you ship to the United States or Europe?

The reference export markets include North America and Europe. Shipping method, Incoterms and compliance documents should be confirmed per order.

Can you provide product photos for my listing?

The final website should collect real product images. Buyers can also request product photos, function diagrams and packaging photos for catalog or listing preparation.

Do you provide inspection photos before shipment?

This can be requested during RFQ. The site should support QC photo, packing photo, carton mark and shipment photo requests.

Can I split MOQ between models?

MOQ split depends on production schedule and product compatibility. Buyers should list desired quantities by model in the RFQ form.

What information should I send for a fast quote?

Send company name, website, target country, model, quantity, certification needs, plug type, logo/package requirements, target price and deadline.

Are the prices on the site final?

No. Prices are reference ranges from the supplied product list. Final price depends on quantity, configuration, packaging, certification support and shipping terms.

Do you support HVAC or commercial project buyers?

Yes. The HVAC duct bipolar ionizer and UV-C disinfection cart pages are designed for project contractors, building system buyers and institutional procurement.

Can you help choose the right CADR or airflow?

Yes. Buyers can share room area, ceiling height and desired ACH target. The sales team can recommend a product direction and confirm CADR requirements.

Do UV-C products require special safety instructions?

Yes. UV-C disinfection products need clear warning labels, manuals and safe operating procedures. Final claims and usage instructions must match product documentation.

Can I download a full product catalog?

The framework includes product pages and PDF spec slots. A full catalog PDF can be added after real images and final parameter tables are confirmed.
