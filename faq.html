Air Purifier OEM FAQ

Buyer-focused questions for distributors, private-label brands and project procurement teams.

What is the MOQ for private-label air purifiers?

MOQ depends on model and customization scope. Standard logo or packaging orders usually start from 50–300 pcs, while ODM or custom tooling requires a separate evaluation.

Can you support CE, FCC, EPA or RoHS documentation?

Documentation support is available for selected models. Final compliance requirements should be confirmed based on model, function, plug type and destination market.

Can we customize logo, color and packaging?

Yes. Logo, shell color, labels, user manual, retail box and carton marks can be customized after sample confirmation.

Do you offer replacement filters?

Yes. Replacement filter bundles can be planned with the initial order to support repeat sales and after-sales service.

How long is production lead time?

Typical production lead time is 30–45 days after deposit and artwork confirmation. Exact timing depends on order quantity, materials and certification documents.

Can we request samples before bulk order?

Yes. Sample orders are recommended for checking airflow, noise level, packaging and local compliance review before bulk production.

Are UV performance claims supported?

UV or sterilization wording should be based on selected model and available test reports. The website uses cautious wording to avoid unsupported claims.

Can you ship to Amazon FBA or overseas warehouses?

Packaging, carton labels and logistics documents can be prepared based on buyer requirements. Final shipping arrangement depends on trade term and forwarder.
