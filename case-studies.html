What a strong case study should contain

A B2B air purifier case study should include buyer type, target region, product category, customization requirement, certificate requirement, sample timeline, production timeline and shipment proof. It does not need to reveal the buyer’s brand name if confidentiality is required. A case can still be credible when it shows the real product, packing photo, market region, quantity range and problem solved.

For example, a private-label home purifier case could explain how the buyer selected a humidification and UV model, tested samples, confirmed packaging artwork and prepared replacement filter SKUs. A European distributor case could focus on CE/RoHS document checking, manual language and retail packaging. A project case could focus on HVAC ionizer or UV-C cart application, inspection and delivery coordination.
