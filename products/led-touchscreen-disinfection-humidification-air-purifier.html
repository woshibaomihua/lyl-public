Buyer qualification notes for LED Touchscreen Series

This model should be evaluated by buyer channel and target market rather than by unit price alone. A distributor may care about carton durability, filter replenishment and local warranty communication. A private-label seller may care about logo area, color box artwork, barcode, instruction manual and product listing photos. A project contractor may care about document pack, installation or usage instructions, bulk delivery and inspection photos before shipment.

Before a bulk order, buyers should confirm the exact electrical version, plug type, voltage, model label, carton marks and certificate documents that match the final SKU. If the buyer plans to sell through Amazon, retail chains, local distributors or government/institutional projects, the quotation should also include packaging size, gross weight, replacement parts, filter SKU and any market-specific warning language.

What to verify before placing an order

  • Final product configuration and available function package.
  • Applicable certificates and document files for the selected model.
  • Sample lead time, courier method and sample cost policy.
  • MOQ by neutral unit, logo customization, color box and full private-label packaging.
  • Carton dimensions, gross weight, packing quantity and shipping mark requirements.
  • Inspection standard, defect handling and shipment photo process.

These checks help both sides avoid misunderstandings.
