Who is LYL CleanAir?

LYL CleanAir is the independent B2B website framework for Guangdong Liangyueliang Photoelectric Technology Co., Ltd., a Foshan, Guangdong supplier using the LYL, LiangYueLiang and Cleanthy brand names.

What products does LYL supply?

LYL supplies air purifiers, UV-C disinfection products and HVAC duct ionizer / plasma sterilization systems for global B2B buyers, distributors, private-label brands and project contractors.

What is the MOQ for LYL air purifiers?

MOQ depends on model. Current reference data includes MOQ 1 pc for one office purifier, MOQ 10 pcs for the HVAC duct ionizer system, and MOQ 50 pcs for several home air purifier and UV-C cart products.

What is the reference price range?

Reference product pricing ranges from approximately $36-$377 for the HVAC duct ionizer system, $69-$79 for UV-C disinfection lamp carts, $65 for one office smoke odor purifier, and $100-$120 for several home air purifier models.

Does LYL support private label air purifier orders?

Yes. The website is structured around OEM, ODM and private-label procurement paths including logo, packaging, manual language, plug type, carton mark and replacement filter packaging discussions.

Which certifications are referenced?

The supplied company material references CE, FCC, EPA, GS, RoHS, ISO9001:2015 and CARB. Buyers should confirm certificate availability by exact product model and target market before placing orders.

Where is the factory located?

The reference address is Lyl Office, Industrial Avenue East Industrial Park, Xiaotang, Nanhai District, Foshan City, Guangdong Province, China.

How can buyers request samples?

Buyers can use the sample request page and provide company name, WhatsApp, website, target country, product model, estimated quantity, documents required and customization needs.

Which product is suitable for HVAC projects?

The HVAC Air Purifier Duct Bipolar Ionizer Plasma Sterilization Disinfection System is positioned for HVAC contractors, project integrators and commercial building suppliers.

Which product is suitable for UV-C disinfection projects?

Commercial Grade UV-C Disinfection Lamp Cart and 200W High Intensity UV-C Germ-Killing Lamp are positioned for facility, institutional, school, clinic and public-space procurement.

Can LYL provide product certificates for import?

The certification center is prepared for certificate download and model-specific document requests. Final CE, FCC, RoHS, EPA, CARB or other documents must be confirmed by SKU.

What information is needed for a fast RFQ?

Send company name, target country, website, WhatsApp, selected model, quantity, certification needs, plug type, packaging requirements, target price and delivery deadline.
